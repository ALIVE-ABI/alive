GOES-R Land Surface Products at AmeriFlux and NEON, Inc. Eddy Covariance Tower Locations

Time-series compilation of GOES-R land surface attributes including visible and near-infrared reflectances, land surface temperature, and downwelling shortwave radiation at 314 ABI fixed grid pixels containing AmeriFlux eddy covariance towers between Aug. 2021-Dec. 2022.

Losos, D., S. Hoffman, and P. Stoy. 2023. GOES-R Land Surface Products at AmeriFlux and NEON, Inc. Eddy Covariance Tower Locations ver 1. Environmental Data Initiative. https://doi.org/10.6073/pasta/f4fc762b96934b4804270c028093681f (Accessed 2023-06-06).
