Advanced Baseline Imager Live Imaging of Vegetated Ecosystems (ALIVE)

Estimating terrestrial carbon dioxide uptake in real time from a geostationary platform

Recent Work

Satellite remote sensing is central to our understanding of the terrestrial carbon cycle and its recent changes. We are working to estimate carbon fluxes in real time using geostationary ("weather") satellites, especially GOES-R (the Geostationary Operational Environmental Satellite - R Series). All of our 'code' is open access and uses open-access 'data', with recent outputs described 'public' We would love to collaborate, please be in touch!
